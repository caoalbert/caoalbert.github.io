Human Mobility

I'm currently working on a project funded by California Air Resource Board (CABR), studying the change in human mobility patterns induced by COVID-19 in California. I designed and implemented the user selection ETL and constructed a state-wide flow network using trino and networkx. I compared and contrasted the flow networks before and after COVID with Louvain's method of community detection.
